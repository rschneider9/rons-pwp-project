Purpose

The purpose of this website is to assist Alisha (my sister) with evolving her cosmetology business, to show the different color combinations and cutting techniques she has to offer. Another purpose to make this website would be an easy way to acquire future clients and book appointments with Alisha.

Audience

The type of audience would be mostly females, both younger and older generations that have a passion for beautiful hair.

Goal

The primary goal of this website would be to have a place for people to connect with Alisha, to ask questions, and/or make appointments. The secondary goals would show pictures of her style that she has to offer, and other clients experiences they've had with Alisha.

Persona

Name: Sandy Flanders

Age 64

Technology

  • Chromebook with 256gb SSD, a basic Samsung cell phone, and running Google Chrome on both.

    • Role

  • Sandy's a general user, nothing more than browsing the web, some social media, and checking emails.

    • Profession

  • Retired, offers help with public schools events, and attends many city councils meetings.

    • Attitudes and Behaviors

  • Sandy likes going out to gatherings, and loves being the center of attention. Having her hair perfect is a must before heading out to one of these events.
  • Sandy has lots of free time, spent mostly watching different trends on the younger kids TikToks, always looking for that new "hip" hairstyle.
  • Sandy is huge on trying to look unique and different, she loves talking about new hair trends with the younger generation. she would rather learn about the hair stylist, and see their work before committing to them
  • Sandy might stumble on this website by word of mouth from people who have loved the work Alisha has given, a search in google, or a local post in the Santa Fe paper.

    • Frustrations and needs

  • Sandy gets frustrated when salons websites make you create an account before booking an appointment or having a good visual of what they offer.
  • Sandy doesn't like just going into a hair salon hoping for the best, she wants a nice visual of the hair stylist types of designs, so she can spend time thinking about the best style that fits her.
  • Sandy gets frustrated when she spends a lot of money on big box hairstyle companies, she would rather that money go to someone local.

    • Goals
  • Sandy's goal of this website would be to quickly see the different style's Alisha has to offer and to be able to make an appointment without having all the fuss of making an account with the website.

    • User Story

  • As someone who always wants to have a unique and trendy hairstyle, I need a way to see the type of style my hairstylist has before choosing where I want to get my hair done.
